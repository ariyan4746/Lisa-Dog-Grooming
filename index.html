Questions Answered

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your pup's first session with Lisa.

Simply give us a call or text at (425) 209-8493. We'll discuss your dog's needs, choose a suitable time, and confirm everything before your appointment. We recommend booking a few days in advance, especially on weekends.
Patience is our superpower. We use calm, gentle handling and take our time at each dog's pace. We never rush or force. For very anxious dogs, we may split grooming over two shorter sessions to build comfort and trust gradually.
We welcome all breeds and sizes — from tiny Chihuahuas to large Golden Retrievers and everything in between. Each coat type is handled with breed-specific techniques and appropriate products for the best results.
Session length depends on the service and your dog's size and coat condition. A simple bath & brush takes 45–75 minutes, while a full grooming package may take up to 2 hours. We'll give you an estimate when you book.
Absolutely. We only use professional-grade, pet-safe, and skin-friendly shampoos and conditioners. If your dog has specific skin sensitivities or allergies, just let us know and we'll choose the most appropriate products.
We are based in Irvine, CA and serve clients throughout the city and surrounding areas. Contact us to confirm availability for your specific location.
